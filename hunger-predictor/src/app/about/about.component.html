About the Mission

This platform is designed to help NGOs and food banks detect hunger hotspots early using data-driven AI forecasts.

Predict food insecurity early

AI + data analytics makes it possible to forecast risk scores for prioritized regions and take preventive action.

Help NGOs allocate resources proactively

Targeted, time-sensitive insight enables more effective deliveries of food and supplies to vulnerable communities.

Data sources

We integrate economic indicators, weather metrics, and supply-chain data to build a comprehensive risk profile.

AI prediction model

An ensemble model considers macroeconomic stress, rainfall anomalies, and local supply volatility to generate a 0–100 risk score.

Impact for humanitarian organizations

A smarter, earlier warning system helps NGOs allocate funding, staff and food reserves where they are most needed.
